Hospital OR Scheduling Optimizer

Formulated a two-stage stochastic Mixed Integer Programming model in GAMSPy using CPLEX to optimize surgery sequencing under real-world uncertainty. Reduced total scheduling costs from $1.26M → $668K — a 47% reduction — with a Value of Stochastic Solution of $591K over the deterministic baseline.

Stack: Python, GAMSPy, CPLEX, Stochastic Optimization, MIP
